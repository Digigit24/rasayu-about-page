Treating Chronic Liver Disease with Ayurvedic Innovation

Recent developments in the understanding of liver cirrhosis and its complications have significantly improved patient life expectancy. However, for many, liver transplantation remains the only curative option—a procedure often hindered by technical challenges and exorbitant costs.

At Rasayu Ayurved Clinic, we are bridging this gap through Complementary and Alternative Medicine (CAM). Our published research demonstrates the profound potential of exclusive Ayurveda therapy consisting of orally administered herbo-mineral formulations.

In a recent case series, male patients diagnosed with radio-logically confirmed liver cirrhosis underwent treatment for 6 to 9 months. The therapeutic outcomes were transformative: patients with gross, moderate, and mild ascites saw complete resolution, alongside significant reductions in total bilirubin levels, showcasing Ayurveda as a viable, non-surgical therapeutic option for liver cirrhosis management.
